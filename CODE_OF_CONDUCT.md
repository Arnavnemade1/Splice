# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to a welcoming, respectful, and productive community for everyone who contributes to Splice.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent while staying accountable for impact.
- Keep technical discussions focused on the work.
- Help newcomers understand project norms and security expectations.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Publishing private information without permission
- Disruptive or bad-faith participation
- Dismissing responsible security concerns

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or restrict participation when behavior violates these expectations.
