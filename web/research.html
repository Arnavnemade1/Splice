Read before believing

Honest scope

  • CPU-scale. Models up to 1B parameters, ~40 prompts per study. These are probes and replications, not frontier claims — the value is that every run is trivially checkable.
  • Local weights only. Nothing here probes a hosted or frontier model's internals. Splice-the-server still makes no claim about the calling model's activations; the lab studies weights you hold.
  • Novel syntheses, not novel primitives. Every experiment is built from standard ingredients — gradients, ablations, the logit lens. The contribution is the packaging and the cross-scale, cross-architecture measurement.
  • The internal trace is richer than the output. A small model can compute an intermediate step it never says out loud — which is the whole reason to look inside.
